Summary: Circuit Crush respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, don't sell your information, and keep your gaming experience private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Circuit Crush collects minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Progress: Your level completion, stars earned, and in-game currency are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Game Center.
  • Game Center Data: If you choose to sign in with Game Center, Apple handles your account information according to their privacy policy.
  • Purchase History: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Enable leaderboards and achievements via Game Center
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve the game experience

3. Data Storage

Your game data is stored:

  • Locally: On your device using standard iOS storage
  • Cloud: Via Apple's Game Center if you choose to sign in (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)

4. Third-Party Services

Circuit Crush uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards, achievements, and cloud saves
  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

Circuit Crush is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not require account creation or personal data entry.

6. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third-party advertisers
  • Track your activity outside the app
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage Game Center data through your iOS Settings
  • Request deletion of any data by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
