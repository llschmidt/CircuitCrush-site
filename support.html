How do I play?

Tap on pipes to rotate them and connect the circuit from Sparkie (the source) to the house (destination). Complete the connection to win!

How do I earn stars?

Complete levels within the par number of moves to earn 3 stars. The fewer moves you use, the more stars you earn!

What are Bolts?

Bolts are the in-game currency. Use them to buy power-ups like Hints, Zaps, and Extra Moves in the shop.

How do I unlock new worlds?

Earn stars to unlock new worlds! Each world requires a certain number of total stars to access.

What do the power-ups do?

üí° Hint: Highlights a pipe that needs to be rotated
‚ö° Zap: Instantly solves one pipe
‚ûï Extra Moves: Adds 5 more moves to your limit

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account & Game Center ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the purchase.

How do I sync my progress across devices?

Sign in to Game Center in Settings. Your progress will automatically sync to iCloud and be available on all your devices.

The game crashed / I found a bug!

We're sorry! Please email us at support@circuitcrush.app with details about what happened, and we'll fix it as soon as possible.

üîß Troubleshooting

Game won't load

Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the problem persists, try restarting your device.

Purchases not appearing

Wait a few moments and try the "Restore Purchases" button in Settings. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.

Game Center not connecting

Check that Game Center is enabled in your device's Settings app, and that you're signed in to your Apple ID.
